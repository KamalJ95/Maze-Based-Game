1) Final Project, maze based game. 

2) A simple 2D Array has been implemented with simple movement mechanics to tranverse the maze.

3) The 'X' are traps and will deal you damage, but they are protecting keys(K)!

4) There are fake keys within the game though, you have limited health!

5) There are also enemies that are protecting keys. These enemies may be more dangerous than you can handle. 

6) There are health packs (H) that you can pick up before facing,these enemies. 

7) These enemies may provide the right key to open the maze, or they might just be fakes again!

8) Play the game with the WASD keys.

9) Find the correct key to open the door and win the maze!
